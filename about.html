About Me

Hi, I'm Henry. Born and raised in New York City, I've always had a camera in my hand, capturing the world around me. From an early age, I've been fascinated by the sights that others might overlook—the hidden beauty in ordinary moments and urban landscapes.

Today, I roam the world with my trusted Olympus Mark IV, documenting life as it unfolds. Street photography isn't just my passion; it's my way of connecting with the world and sharing perspectives that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Through this website, I invite you to see the world through my eyes—a collection of candid moments, urban textures, and the beautiful chaos of city life. Welcome to my visual journey.

I have a few other focuses and pursuits which I've added here.
